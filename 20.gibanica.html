Gibanica

Fay Delaney

Ingredients
  • Cottage cheese - 600 g
  • Elmea double cream (half the tub)
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon of baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 500g filo pastry
  • Toppings:Elmea double cream (half the tub)
    1 egg
    1 teaspoon salt
    • Method

    1. Heat the oven to 200 C.
    2. Mix all the ingredients for the mixture EXCEPT the filo pastry. The order is not very important but it works well if you add cheese, then cream, salt, baking powder then eggs. When all is well mixed, use one sheet of filo pastry to line the tray and tear the rest in pieces and add to the mixture, sort off staring continuously, so they are well mixed in. Once all the filo pastry is in, pour it into the tray and spread evenly.
    3. Now, add the egg and salt to the remainder of Elmea cream and mix well. Pour on top the mixture that is in the baking tray.
    4. Put in the oven on the middle shelf for 20-ish min. Check after 15 to see how it is going.
