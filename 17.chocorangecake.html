Simple Chocolate Orange Marble Cake

Vincent Karthaus

Ingredients
  • 225g self-raising flour
  • 225 butter
  • 225g caster sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 large/2 small oranges
  • A splash of milk
  • 3 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 100g chocolate, optional
    • Method

    1. Preheat the oven to 180Â°C
    2. Cream the butter and sugar together
    3. Add the eggs, two at a time, and beat
    4. Fold in the flour, sifted, a little at a time
    5. Separate into two equal bowls
    6. In the first add the cocoa powder, chocolate (if wanted) and enough milk to make the batter only just pourable
    7. In the second bowl add all of the zest and of the orange(s), and a little flour, to bring it to a similar consistency as the chocolate half
    8. Pour in a small, alternating layers of the chocolate mix and the orange mix into a loaf tin, then drag a skewer through a few times to marble it (don't do too much marbling otherwise you might just get a blend)
    9. Bake in the oven for 40 minutes, or until a skewer comes out clean
    10. Serve warm with some warm cream or ice cream
