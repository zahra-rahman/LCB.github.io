Pink Lemonade Meringues

Rosa Thompson

Ingredients
  • 3 egg whites
  • 225g caster sugar
  • Zest of one lemon
  • 150g raspberries
  • 1tbsp icing sugar
  • 1tsp lemon juice
  • 300ml whipping cream
    • Method

    1. Preheat oven to 160°c and line ½ baking trays
    2. Whip the egg whites until they form stiff peaks
    3. Add the sugar bit by bit, the mixture should be stiff and shiny
    4. Mix in the lemon zest
    5. Pipe the meringues onto the baking tray in little swirls
    6. Turn the oven down to 140°c and bake the meringues for an hour.
    7. Whip the cream
    8. Blend the raspberries, icing sugar and lemon juice into a puree
    9. Sift the puree into the icing sugar, being careful to make sure there are no bits
    10. Once the meringues have cooled, sandwich them together with some of the raspberry cream.
    11. Enjoy :)
