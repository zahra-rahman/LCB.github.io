Raspberry or Caramel Brownies

Madeleine Lieberman

Ingredients
  • 100g butter
  • 100g dark chocolate (70%)
  • 3 small eggs/ 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 200g sugar
  • 100g flour
  • 2 pinches of salt
  • OPTIONAL: 10-20 raspberries or½ tin of caramel
    • Method

    1. Preheat the oven to 170c fan
    2. Melt the chocolate and butter together until fully melted and mixed together.
    3. In the bowl, add the sugar to the mix and stir well
    4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs, vanilla and salt.
    5. Add this to the mix
    6. Next, sieve the flour in bit by bit until it’s fully mixed in
    7. Then get a 20cmx20cm square tin, line it with greaseproof paper, and pour the brownie mixture in
    8. If you want, you can put some raspberries in or some caramel (make sure the caramel is quite liquidy so it’s easy to pour (you can do this by adding hot water)). If adding caramel, you can swirl it around so it is evenly spread.
    9. Put the brownies in the oven for 17-20 minutes.
    10. Leave it to cool for 1-2 hours.
