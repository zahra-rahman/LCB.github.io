Cinnamon Rolls

Samuel Ogbole

Ingredients
  • 200g of Cinnamon Kit
  • 100g of cinnamon sugar
  • 75g of icing powder
  • 50g of very soft unsalted butter
  • 30ml of semi-skimmed milk
  • 45 ml water and 1 medium egg
    • Method

    1. Empty bun mix sachet into a bowl then fold in 20g of the butter, 1 egg and 45ml (3 tbsp) water until fully combined. Knead for 8 minutes until it forms a smooth elastic dough.
    2. Roll it out approx. 30cm long and 15cm wide, then spread the remaining 30g butter over the dough and sprinkle with the cinnamon sugar. Roll up the long side of the dough into a swiss roll shape, then cut it into 8 even pieces
    3. Place each of the buns with the sliced side facing upwards in a tin lined in greaseproof paper. Cover the tin with cling film and leave it to prove in a warm place for 45 mins
    4. Preheat the oven to 200℃/ 375℉ / Fan 180℃ Gas Mark 6. Remove the cling film and bake in the oven for 20-25 minutes until well risen and golden brown
    5. Be careful when removing the tray from over. Use mitts or a cloth to remove the pan. Leave rolls to cool for 5 mins
    6. Mix the icing with milk and drizzle over the cooled buns. Pull apart and tuck in!
