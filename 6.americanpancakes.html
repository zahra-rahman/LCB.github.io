American Pancakes

Flore Servant

Ingredients
  • 250 g flour
  • 30 g sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 10g baking powder
  • 65 g butter
  • A pinch of salt
  • 300 ml milk
    • Method

    1. Melt the butter.
    2. Put the flour, baking powder and sugar into a mixing bowl. Stir well.
    3. Add the eggs to the flower mixture and stir.
    4. Mix in the melted butter, and then gradually add the milk to avoid any lumps
    5. Leave the pancake batter in the fridge for at least one hour.
    6. In a warmed, and lightly-oiled pan, cook the pancakes. Use the same technique as you would for normal pancakes, only make them smaller.
    7. Enjoy!
