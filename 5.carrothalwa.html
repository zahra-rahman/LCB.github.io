Carrot Halwa

Mehul Srivastava

Ingredients
  • 1kg Carrot
  • 200ml Condensed milk
  • 2sp Ghee/ butter
  • Pinch of cardamom powder
  • 4-5 Chopped almond and cashew nuts
    • Method

    1. Wash and peel the carrots.
    2. Cut them into small pieces and steam it in a pan until they become soft.
    3. Once soft ,mash them and add ghee ,cook until all the water disappears.
    4. Once all the juices are gone add condensed milk and cardamom powder and stir until everything is mixed well.
    5. Stir for another ten minutes and then add chopped cashew nuts and almonds.
    6. Now you can switch off the heat and the halwa is ready to eat.
