Nola's Homemade Poke

Ingredients
  • Long grain Rice
  • Some form of Meat or Tofu to top (Example: pork dumplings)
  • Around 5 different types of vegetables â€“ examples below:
  • Carrots, Cucumber, Bell peppers, Edamame, Cherry Tomatoes, Nori (Seaweed),
  • Sesame seeds
  • Mirin and / or rice wine vinegar (optional)
  • Chilli Sauce (Example: Siracha)
    • Method

    1. Cook your rice for about 10 minutes and then set aside and leave to cool
    2. If your topping requires cooking, cook that then also set it aside to cool
    3. Peel, Chop or Cook your vegetables as necessary then place in small piles around the edge/s of your plate/s.
    4. If using, mix your rice in with your mirin and rice wine vinegar. Place it in the middle of your plate.
    5. Place your topping on the top of your plate and dress with sesame seeds and chilli sauce (If using)
    6. Serve and Enjoy!!!
